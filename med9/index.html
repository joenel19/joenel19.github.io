The lion

The lion is a large cat. It has a muscular body and round ears. It also has a hairy tuft at the end of it's tail. Adult male lions are larger than female lions. Groups of female lions hunt together. Social species form groups called prides. Lions are the only cats that live in groups. Female lions are the main hunters. Lions have a terrific night vision. The lion's worst enemy is the hyena.
